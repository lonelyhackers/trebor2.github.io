Hi.
This is a website about exoskeletons - specifically, my exoskeletons. An exoskeleton is a wearable robot, in this case, for paralyzed patients. Their purpose is to restore mobility.
In essence, an exoskeleton gives users more control, and more choice, in life.
This portfolio should take about 8 minutes to read. There is a preface, two galleries with captions, and a discussion. If you’re limited on time, browse through some of the photos. No one’s ever regretted it.
