Hi.
This is a website about exoskeletons - specifically, my exoskeletons. An exoskeleton is a wearable robot, in this case, for paralyzed patients. Their purpose is to restore mobility. In essence, an exoskeleton gives users more control, and more choice, in life.
This archive should take about 8 minutes to go through. There is a preface, two galleries with captions, and a discussion. If you’re limited on time, browse through some of the photos (I recommend part two.) No one’s ever regretted it.
